Section 7 · Sacred trust

Privacy policy.

We treat your data the way we treat the forest — minimally disturbed, ethically harvested, never sold. This page explains what we collect, why, and what your rights are.

Last updated 23 May 2026 · Effective immediately
⟡ In one sentence
We store only what your shopping cart and saved formulas need to work — no analytics, no tracking pixels, no selling your data, ever.

Who we are

Fungai Art is an independent artistic and herbal practice operated by Robin Fungi and Steph Teÿæ. Contact: robin@fungai.art. We are the data controller for personal information collected through this website (fungai.art).

What we collect

  • Local browser storage (localStorage) — your shopping cart, any herbal formulas you save in the Herbal Engine, and your cookie-notice acknowledgement. This data never leaves your browser. We don't see it.
  • Order information (only if you purchase) — name, email, shipping address, items ordered. Used to fulfil and ship your order. Stored securely; deleted on request.
  • Newsletter email (only if you subscribe) — email address you submit to the newsletter form. Used only to send the monthly Field Notes. Unsubscribe anytime, instantly.
  • Payment information — processed by Stripe. Fungai Art never sees your card number. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

What we do not collect

  • No Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No behavioural profiling or cross-site tracking
  • No fingerprinting
  • No data sold to anyone, for any reason, ever

Third-party services

The site uses these external services. Each handles data only as needed for the function listed:

  • Netlify — hosting and form submissions. Server logs may retain IP addresses briefly for security. netlify.com/privacy
  • Stripe — payment processing (only when you check out). stripe.com/privacy
  • Open-Meteo — weather data for the Foraging Map. No personal data sent. CC BY 4.0 licensed.
  • GBIF — biodiversity observations for the Foraging Map. Public CC0 data. No personal data sent.
  • CARTO basemaps — map tiles. Loaded directly by your browser; CARTO may log the tile requests.

Cookies & local storage

We use the bare minimum:

  • fungai_cart — your shopping cart (localStorage, browser-only)
  • fungai_formulas — formulas you save in the Herbal Engine (localStorage, browser-only)
  • fungai_cookie_ack — remembers that you've seen this notice (localStorage, browser-only)
  • Stripe session cookies — only set when you check out, for fraud prevention

Clearing your browser data removes all of the above. We do not use marketing or analytics cookies of any kind.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/UK (and we extend these rights to everyone), you can:

  • Access — request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Correct — ask us to fix anything inaccurate
  • Delete — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in machine-readable form
  • Object — stop us using your data for any specific purpose
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe, remove yourself, anytime

To exercise any of these, email robin@fungai.art. We'll respond within 30 days — usually within 48 hours.

Data retention

  • Order records — kept for 7 years (Swedish accounting law requirement), then deleted
  • Newsletter email — until you unsubscribe
  • Local browser data — until you clear it or revisit this site after a long absence

Security

The site is served over HTTPS only. Payments are processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1). We do not store payment data on our servers. Newsletter and form submissions are stored by Netlify with industry-standard encryption.

Children

The site is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Some herbs documented in our materia medica are restricted for adult use only.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, the "Last updated" date at the top will change and a notice will appear on the homepage. Material changes will be announced via the newsletter.

Get in touch

Questions, complaints, or data requests:

robin@fungai.art

You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority. In Sweden, that's Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY): imy.se
