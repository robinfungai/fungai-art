Onboarding · 10 minutes

Welcome to
The Mycelium.

You've been invited into the Fungai Art network. This page walks you through the four simple steps to join — installing a wallet, switching to the right network, sharing your address, and waiting for your Hyphae to spore in. If you get stuck on any step, write to robin@fungai.art.

"You're not buying access. You're agreeing to share threads with us — to give what you have, to receive what flows. Hyphae are the language of that exchange."

The four steps

From here to your first Hyphae.

1
Install MetaMask
~5 minutes · free · browser extension

MetaMask is the wallet that will hold your Hyphae. It's a free browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, Edge). When you install it, it will give you a 12-word seed phrase — write that down on paper and keep it safe. Never type it into any website. The seed phrase is the only thing that proves the wallet is yours.

Install MetaMask →
2
Switch to Polygon
~2 minutes · one-time setup

Hyphae live on Polygon (a fast, low-cost network). MetaMask opens on the Ethereum mainnet by default — you need to switch. Click the network dropdown at the top of MetaMask → Add network → Add manually → enter:

Then switch your active network to Polygon Mainnet. You don't need to buy any MATIC — Robin pays the gas fees when sporing Hyphae to you.

3
Send your wallet address
~1 minute · one email

Click your account name at the top of MetaMask — it will say something like 0x7a3...c0d — and copy the full address (starts with 0x, 42 characters total). Then email it to Robin:

Email Robin your address →
4
Wait for your first sporing
~1 day · automatic

Robin will spore Hyphae into your wallet within 24 hours of receiving your address. You'll see them in MetaMask under the Polygon network. The contract is currently being prepared — until it goes live, your wallet is queued and the first sporing happens at deployment.

Once you have Hyphae, you can connect your wallet on /members to see your balance, browse /draw to redeem extracts and ceremonies, and read more about how the network breathes at /sporing.

After your first Hyphae
