Shoot day 1 — June 7
The Medicine Series
iPhone + natural light
0 of 7 shots
Shoot in golden hour or by a north-facing window. Tincture bottles catch light beautifully on a dark surface — try bark or slate underneath.
Block 3 hours. First hour setup and stills, second hour video and texture shots, third hour review and upload to Drive.
Bring props: dried herbs, dark cloth, wooden bowl, ceramic cup, mortar and pestle. These appear in multiple shots.

Ceremony + Forest
Mix — outdoors
0 of 6 shots
Early morning or late afternoon for forest light. Bring a small ritual setup: cloth, dried herbs, a bowl. The tablescape can be made anywhere.
Overcast days are ideal — soft even light, no harsh shadows. Do not cancel a shoot day just because it is not sunny.
For the anonymous person shot, back to camera works perfectly. No face, full atmosphere. Simple, protected, powerful.
