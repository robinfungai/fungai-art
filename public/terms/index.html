Foraging safety

The Foraging Map shows habitats, traditional harvest locations, and community observations. The map is approximate. Real-world plant identification requires expert knowledge.

  • Never eat anything you cannot identify with 100% certainty. Several plants and mushrooms in our materia medica have lethal lookalikes.
  • Respect private property, national parks, and protected species lists.
  • The right of public access (allemansrätten in Sweden, etc.) varies by country. Know your local rules.
  • Forage sustainably — never take more than 20% of any patch.
  • Some species we document (e.g. Amanita muscaria, Calamus, Lobelia, Ephedra) are regulated or restricted in some jurisdictions. Know your local laws.

By using the Foraging Map and related guides, you accept full responsibility for your own safety and the consequences of your foraging activity.
