The format

Living drinks, kegged.

Each batch begins as a fermenting mother — vinegar, switchel, kombucha-base or hydromel — and is then layered with the same wild-foraged extracts and spagyric essences that go into the Fungai Art apothecary. The drink keeps fermenting in the keg. The medicine doesn’t go to sleep.

How it’s made

From forest to keg.

01 · Source
Wild-foraged
Plants from the same network as the apothecary: Sweden, Berlin, Riviera, Cappadocia, Beirut. Real coordinates, real seasons.
02 · Extract
Spagyric & cold
Mother tinctures + cold-pressed concentrates prepared in the Berlin lab using the same five-method extraction frame.
03 · Ferment
Living mother
A switchel, hydromel or kombucha mother holds the extract during a 2-6 week secondary ferment. The medicine ages with the drink.
04 · Tune
111 Hz pass
Each keg is given a final 111 Hz resonance pass — the same frequency the apothecary’s elixirs receive before they leave.
05 · Tap
Pour on location
Kegs travel to ceremonies, residencies and partner dispensaries. The drink is served on tap so the fermentation stays alive to the last pour.
