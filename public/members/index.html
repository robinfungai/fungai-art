How to join

Becoming a member.

1
Install MetaMask (or any EIP-1193 wallet — Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, Trust). It's a browser extension. Free. Five minutes. metamask.io
2
Add Polygon network to your wallet. $HYPHA lives on Polygon mainnet (chain ID 137) — gas fees are fractions of a cent.
3
Reach out to Robin — email robin@fungai.art with your wallet address and a short note about why you'd like to be part of the circle. Membership is invitation-based for the founding cohort.
4
Receive your $HYPHA. Robin mints directly to your wallet. Connect on this page to verify — gated content becomes visible immediately.
5
Belong. Members can transfer or gift their tokens to others, but $HYPHA is not listed on any DEX or CEX. It's a soulbound-by-spirit, free-by-spirit token of belonging.
