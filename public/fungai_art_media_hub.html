Workflow — How We Work Together

1
Add footage to Drive
Drop photos and video into /fungAI art/footage as you shoot. No need to organize — just upload and I can browse it.
2
Monthly planning session here
We sit down together in Claude and build the next month's calendar, captions, and reel scripts based on what's in Drive and what's coming up.
3
Schedule via Metricool or Later
Copy captions and schedule posts. Reels get scripted here and edited in CapCut or Premiere. One session per week, 20 minutes max.
4
Beacons stays live and updated
We update the Beacons link-in-bio to match current campaigns. Events, tincture drops, membership launches.
5
Review and grow
Each month we look at what worked and evolve the strategy. The living archive deepens with every post.
